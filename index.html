About Me

Hello, I'm Li, a male massage therapist registered with the College of Massage Therapists of Ontario. With over 2 years of professional experience, I specialize in providing therapeutic massage treatments to help you achieve relaxation, pain relief, and improved well-being.

I practice from my comfortable home clinic located conveniently near Scarborough Town Centre, offering a peaceful environment for your treatment sessions. My approach combines technical expertise with a personalized touch to address your specific needs.

As a healthcare professional, I maintain the highest standards of cleanliness, confidentiality, and therapeutic care. Your health and comfort are my top priorities during every session.
